About

Introduction

I am a mechanical and software engineer working at Aerospace Systems and Components, a manufacturer of aerospace pneumatic components. I have a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Benedictine College in Atchison, KS. I previously worked as a software engineer for Tallac Networks, a software defined networking startup, and as an Undergraduate Engineering Assistant at Benedictine College. I was the American Society of Mechanical Engineers Chapter Secretary for Benedictine College from 2019-2020. I am a member of the National Association of Rocketry and worked with a group of students and professors to build and launch a high power rocket at Benedictine with the ultimate goal of starting a rocketry program. Aside from many carpentry, electronics, engineering, and/or coding projects, some of which are shown in the projects page, my hobbies include sailing, hiking, and photography.
